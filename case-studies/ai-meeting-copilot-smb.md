# AI Meeting Copilot for SMB Teams

## Problem
Small teams spend too much time in meetings and still leave without clear decisions, owners, or follow-ups. Existing notes are often passive summaries rather than execution tools.

## User
Primary user: team leads and operators in SMBs who need meetings to convert into accountable action.

## Insight
The real value is not transcript compression. It is decision capture plus follow-through.

## Proposed Solution
An AI meeting copilot that identifies decisions, unresolved questions, owners, due dates, and follow-up risks, then turns them into structured post-meeting actions.

## MVP Scope
- In scope: meeting summary, action item extraction, owner tagging, follow-up recap
- Out of scope: full calendar automation, advanced analytics, multilingual optimization

## User Flow
1. Meeting is recorded
2. AI generates summary + decisions + tasks
3. User reviews and confirms outputs
4. Follow-up digest is shared with the team

## Risks
- Incorrect owner assignment may break trust
- Hallucinated decisions could create operational confusion
- Users may not want AI in sensitive meetings

## KPIs
- North star: % of meetings resulting in confirmed action items
- Activation: first meeting summarized and shared
- Engagement: weekly meetings processed per team
- Retention: teams using the tool 4 weeks later
- Quality: correction rate on decisions / action items

## Launch Plan
Start with small internal teams and founder-led companies. Position around clarity and accountability, not just note-taking.

## Tradeoffs
The MVP optimizes for reliability and user review instead of full end-to-end automation.
