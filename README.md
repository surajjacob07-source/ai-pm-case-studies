# AI PM Case Studies

A portfolio repo of product management case studies focused on AI features, workflows, and product bets.

## What this repo demonstrates
- Problem framing
- User segmentation
- MVP thinking
- Prioritization and tradeoffs
- KPI design
- Launch planning for AI products

## Structure
- `case-studies/` — individual PM case studies
- `prds/` — concise AI feature PRDs
- `templates/` — reusable PM templates

## Starter case studies
- AI Meeting Copilot for SMB teams
- AI Inbox Triage for founders and operators
- AI Job Search Copilot for AI PM candidates

## Intended audience
Recruiters, founders, hiring managers, and collaborators evaluating AI product management thinking.
